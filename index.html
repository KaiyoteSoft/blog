Why fish?

It's called fishing, not catching

Fishing motivates me to explore new locations, hike to remote destinations and meet new people. While the goal is always to catch fish, the simple process of going outside and tackling a new challenge is rewarding in itself. I've walked over a frozen lake, hiked in darkness and learned to freedive in pursuit of fish. Every fishing trip is an adventure. Every adventure enriches my life.
